I’m an Assistant Professor in the School of Computer and Information Engineering at Kwangwoon University. I received my B.S. in Mathematics and Computer Science from Sungkyunkwan University. I also received my Ph.D. in the Information Security Group (ISG) at Sungkyunkwan University, where I was advised by Prof. Dongho Won and Prof. Hyoungshick Kim. My research interests include Cyber Security, Machine Learning, and User Authentication.

In our research group, we are always looking for excellent undergraduate and graduate students.

I want to help many people around the world through research.
